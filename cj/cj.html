Meet our ML based ChatBot.

Call me CJ

Use keywords when you're being tempted (mayday, IceCream ahead, craving alert etc.) and CookieJar uses ML/AI based dialog to walk you back from the brink of the endugence cliff. A recipe based on counseling Psychology, with a dash of predictive analytics, makes for a delicious side.

Simple UX

Simple user interface, chat-based, location-based, it learns preferences, restrictions, allergies. It's non-intrusive, no calorie counting, can log preferences through chat.

Across devices

Use it at work, home or out. Coaching and food suggestions when asked. Location based user-specific recommendation. Across multiple platforms (App/web, FB Messenger, Alexa, Google Assistant).
