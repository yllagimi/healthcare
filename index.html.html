R2 media labs

We're a collective of do-gooders, working together on a few ideas.

Our goal is to apply data science to help people make healthier decisions. At the hospital, so providers know their patient before seeing them or at the store, so people can better choose what's good for them. We also strive to connect disperate data sources to answer health and wellbeing related questions.
