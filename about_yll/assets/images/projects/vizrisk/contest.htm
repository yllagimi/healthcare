Predicted Healthcare Costs per Year

As you change your risk factors your disease probabilities will change, and you will see an increase (red extension of the bar) in predicted healthcare costs for individuals with your characteristics and your risk factors, based on the 2012 Medical Expenditure Panel Survey.
