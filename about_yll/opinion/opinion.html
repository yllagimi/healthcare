About this page

I'm a health services epidemiologist with about 10 years experience in studying patterns of injury and health care use among civilians and military populations. Currently I'm working on large cohort data, looking at care patterns and pathways, predictors of visits, misdiagnoses and such fun stuff. I also spend some time modeling the impact of state policies on injuries among teens , the elderly and military.
